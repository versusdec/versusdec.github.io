FAQ

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?
Choose a tariff convenient for you - pay by the minute or take advantage of the package offers. If you need a scooter for a one-time trip, it is more convenient to use a per-minute payment. current tariff - 5 cents. per minute of movement and 1.5 minutes in parking mode. If you need to move a short distance, but several times during the day, it is possible it will be more profitable to use the daily rate, which is 20 usd. per day (limit mileage included in the price of 100 km, mileage above is paid additionally). New tariffs will be introduced in accordance with the requirements and wishes of customers. We are waiting for your suggestions!
WHERE YOU CAN RIDE?
A scooter is a technique for short trips, but if you wish, you can move dozens kilometers. The permitted zone for driving and parking is marked in the application. Always park scooter in a public place allowed for two-wheelers.
WHO REFUELS AND PAYS FOR GASOLINE?
You do not need to worry about refueling, the operator will take care of it himself. If happened to refuel the scooter on your own - we will fully compensate your fuel costs with bonus to your account in the application.
AND IF YOU HAVE NO DRIVING EXPERIENCE?
The scooter is very easy to operate: throttle and brake handles, automatic transmission, light weight. Not harder than a bike. That is why this type of transport is so popular among all ages. If a If you are inexperienced, we suggest watching a short training video on tour-de-jour.ru. Also our managers will be happy to give you a free short driving lesson.
HOW TO START THE ENGINE?
To start the engine: (starting on the center stand is allowed)
  • in the application, turn on the "ignition", the dashboard will turn on
  • squeeze out any brake (preferably both)
  • click on the starter button.
    When starting a cold engine, do not touch the throttle. After starting, let the engine warm up at high speed for 1-2 minutes. If the engine does not start immediately, check that the fuel and battery gauges raised and the engine stop button is not activated
  • smoothly turn the throttle stick to the maximum and also smoothly return it to its original position Try to start the engine again without touching the throttle
WHAT TO DO IF THE STARTER DOES NOT TURN?
Make sure that:
  • The ignition is on and the fuel and battery gauges are up and the engine stop button is not activated
  • Both brake levers pressed
    If, when the starter button is pressed, it does not crank the engine or does it very slowly - The battery is dead. But there is a chance to start it with a kick starter. This is not difficult.
  • Put the scooter on the center stand.
  • Make sure the ignition is on and the fuel and battery gauges are up and the stop button engine not activated
  • When starting the engine with a kick starter, the brake levers do NOT need to be applied.
  • Start the engine with a quick movement by pressing the kick starter with your foot.
HOW TO FILL THE SCOOTER BY YOURSELF?
Do not let the fuel in the tank run completely. The volume of the tank is about 5 liters.
For refueling you need:
  • put the scooter on the center stand
  • raise the seat
  • unscrew the fuel tank cap
  • fill with Gasoline 92.
HOW TO DRESS YOUR SCOOTER RIDES?
A helmet is not only a requirement of traffic rules and protection in case of an accident, but also protection from insects, stones from under the wheels, wind and bad weather. Long sleeves and trousers will protect against sunburn during the day and against the cold in the evening. For trips at night, even in summer, it is better to have a jacket or windbreaker with you, because. moving forward will much colder from the headwind than just for a walk. If the jacket is not needed, it will not bother you if you put it in the scooter case.
What is the maximum speed the scooter can accelerate to and what is the maximum weight it can carry?
The maximum speed of the scooter is 50 km / h, the maximum load capacity is 150 kg, but if your the route lies along steep slopes, the maximum load capacity is 100 kg.
