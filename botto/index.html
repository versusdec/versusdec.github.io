Where to begin?

Minimum deposit
We do not have a minimum deposit amount. To fully test all the features of the platform, we We recommend replenishing your account with at least 1000 dollars.
What functionality is working now
At the moment, only the Botto Communications application is working and available in it: a voice robot, tracking system and auto funnel constructor.
What functionality is planned
As part of Botto Communication, we plan to launch the following functionality in the near future: constructor mini-sites, omnichannel communications and a dialogue system. In the future, 2 more applications will be available: Botto Leads and Botto Broker.
What are applications?
Applications are separate software modules with an individual set of functionality. When you create a project, you you can connect the applications you need. Thus, in the project you will use only those platform features that you really need.
How can I quickly attract new customers?
With the help of the Botto Platform, in just 10 minutes you can attract new customers to your business. Create a voice or combined mailing, upload the database and set up processing scenarios. The system will be automated interact with potential customers and warmly convey to you in a convenient way.
What to do if there is no base?
If you do not have a base, write to us and we will recommend you partners who will promptly provide you with bases from open sources.
I use SMS and everything suits me
Using our platform, you get the opportunity to inform customers 5-10 times cheaper. At the same time, you interactive communication options will be available.
What about integration?
We have already implemented integrations with most popular software platforms such as Bitrix24, AMOCRM and etc. Also, we have developed a powerful and documented API. When replenishing the balance in the amount of more than 30,000 dollars one-time we are ready to implement free integration with almost any software platform.
